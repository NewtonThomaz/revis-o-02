tomb raider

The game tells the story of a very young Lara Croft, who, after being shipwrecked, finds herself alone and isolated on a Japanese island, only to discover later that some of her friends and colleagues are still alive. She has already proven herself to be a survivor, but she is on the verge of an extraordinary adventure.
